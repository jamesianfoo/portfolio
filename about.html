Strategic Product & Design Leader | d.MBA | Enterprise Platform Transformation

I'm a Product Designer with over a decade of experience helping organisations make sense of complexity — across enterprise SaaS, financial services, and developer tooling.

I do my best work when the problem is messy and the stakes are high. Ambiguous briefs, competing priorities, organisations mid-transformation — that's where I tend to find clarity that others miss.

I hold a Design MBA (d.MBA) because I think the best design work is inseparable from business strategy. If you can't connect it to value, it won't survive a boardroom. It sharpened how I think about tradeoffs, cost, and what it actually takes to get good design to stick.

People matter to me as much as the work itself. I've spent a lot of time mentoring designers, getting involved in hiring, and trying to be the kind of leader I wish I'd had earlier. If you want to talk design, strategy, or just swap notes — I'm always up for it.
